Overview

wheresTHAT ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • GPS Location: When you grant permission, we collect your real-time location to:
    • Show your position on the map
    • Track your flight or driving route
    • Find nearby points of interest
    • Calculate distances
  • Location Control: You can deny or revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Location Storage: Your location data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers

User-Entered Information

  • Flight numbers you enter (stored locally only)
  • Saved locations and notes (stored locally only)
  • Trip history (stored locally only)

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports (for debugging)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most)

What We DON'T Do

  • ‚ùå We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • ‚ùå We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • ‚ùå We do NOT collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers (unless you contact support)
  • ‚ùå We do NOT upload your location history to our servers
  • ‚ùå We do NOT require account creation

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide app functionality:

  • Display your position on maps
  • Show relevant points of interest near you
  • Calculate distances and routes
  • Save your favorite locations (locally on your device)
  • Track and display your trip history (locally on your device)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

wheresTHAT uses the following third-party services:

Google Maps Platform

  • Used for: Map display, satellite imagery, place information
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Note: Your location queries are sent to Google Maps API to retrieve map tiles and place data

Google Places API

  • Used for: Finding nearby points of interest, place details, and photos
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Expo Platform

  • Used for: App updates and crash reporting
  • Privacy Policy: Expo Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your saved locations, trip history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not automatically back up your data to cloud servers
  • Data Loss: If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be deleted
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures to protect data transmission (HTTPS encryption)

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app in the Settings screen
  • Delete: Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
  • Opt-Out: Deny location permissions at any time (some features will be limited)
  • Data Portability: Export your saved locations from the app (feature coming soon)

Children's Privacy

wheresTHAT is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information submission.

Location Permissions

How We Request Location Access:

  • When you select "I'm Flying" or "On the Road", we request location permission
  • We explain why we need location access before requesting it
  • You can deny permission and still use limited features (e.g., demo mode, manual location search)
  • Location is only accessed while the app is actively in use (no background tracking)

International Users

wheresTHAT is available worldwide. Your location data is processed on your device and never transmitted to our servers, regardless of your location.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying an in-app notification when significant changes occur
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
